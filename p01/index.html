Three-layer structure: cold acquisition, warm retargeting, lookalike audiences from enrolled students. Meta handles interest and demographic signals. LinkedIn handles job title and company targeting.

Target Audiences

  • Meta: Software engineers by interest + engagement with AI/ML content in the last 60 days
  • Meta: Engineers at companies with 500+ employees, ages 27–42
  • LinkedIn: "Software Engineer," "Senior SWE," "Staff Engineer" at Series B+ companies
  • LinkedIn: Engineers at Big Tech (Google, Meta, Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, Nvidia, OpenAI)
